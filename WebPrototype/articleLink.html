H2 TITLE INTRODUCING THE READER TO THE ARTICLE

  • Links appear in a light blue color to easily allow readers to identify where they can click

  • Verdana font is used to be as multiple sources agree it is the most readable on mobile and computer screens.

  • Particular focus is placed on the mobile design, so every aspect of an article page is mobile responsive.

  • Images can be displayed in a variety of different ways, two per row, four per row, two columns of three images etc.

  • The article pages are purposely uncluttered as a user simply wants to get their information as quickly and as easily as possible, that is why no sidebar navigation exists and why images/videos are put in their own seperate rows
